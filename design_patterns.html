1 why use design patterns? benefits

  • speed up the development process by providing tested, proven development paradigms
  • reusing skeletons prevents subtle issues that can cause major problems
  • improves code readability
  • applicable to a boarder range of problems, not tied to a particular problem,
    • can be improved over time, not a ad-hoc design
